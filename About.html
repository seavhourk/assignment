About Us

Welcome to our Capybarastores! Here, we bring the warmth and cuteness of the world’s friendliest animal—the capybara—into our products. Known for their gentle and stable nature, capybaras are always friendly and easygoing, often becoming the "living sofa" for other animals. Our goal is to bring you a special sense of joy and comfort, whether you're studying, working, or living. The adorable capybara image will accompany you through every beautiful moment. Our Products Our shop primarily offers two categories of products: Stationery: We provide a variety of capybara-themed stationery, including notebooks, pencil cases, stickers, and sticky notes. All our stationery is beautifully designed and filled with capybara elements. Whether you're a student, office worker, or stationery enthusiast, you'll find something you'll love here. Accessories: Our accessory collection also revolves around the capybara theme, featuring keychain charms, hair accessories, jewelry, and other small items. Each piece is carefully designed to add a unique and cute touch to your daily style. Our Philosophy We believe that every little detail in life can be made more beautiful and fun. By incorporating the friendly, warm, and emotionally stable nature of capybaras into our products, we aim to bring warmth and endless joy to each of our customers. Thank you for choosing our shop. Let’s discover more beauty together in the world of capybaras!
